Academic Papers

Attention Is All You Need

Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A. N., Kaiser, L., & Polosukhin, I. (2017). Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems, 30, 5998–6008.

Introduced the Transformer model, completely replacing recurrent layers with multi-head self-attention mechanisms.

62,000+ Citations Self-Attention Transformer PDF ↗

BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding

Devlin, J., Chang, M. W., Lee, K., & Toutanova, K. (2018). arXiv preprint arXiv:1810.04805.

Introduced BERT, a bidirectional representation model pre-trained on MLM and NSP tasks, setting state-of-the-art across 11 tasks.

45,000+ Citations BERT Pre-training PDF ↗

Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space

Mikolov, T., Chen, K., Corrado, G., & Dean, J. (2013). arXiv preprint arXiv:1301.3781.

Introduced the Skip-gram and Continuous Bag-of-Words (CBOW) architectures for training high-quality vector representations (Word2Vec).

28,000+ Citations Word2Vec Embeddings PDF ↗
