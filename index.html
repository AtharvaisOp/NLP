Why These 8 NLP Topics?

Each topic directly maps to a step in the महापल्स Marathi Sentiment Analysis pipeline.

🧹

Text Cleaning & Noise Removal

Strip URLs, HTML, emojis, and non-Devanagari characters from raw Marathi social media data.

✂️

Sentence Segmentation & Tokenization

Detect Marathi sentence boundaries (using danda ।) and apply MahaBERT WordPiece subword tokenization.

🔤

Stop-Word Removal & Lemmatization

Filter L3Cube's 400 Marathi stop words and reduce inflected forms to dictionary base lemmas.

🔢

Contextual Embeddings

Generate 768-dim context-sensitive word vectors using MahaBERT's encoder — resolving Marathi polysemy.

🤖

MahaBERT / IndicBERT

Fine-tune a pre-trained Marathi transformer on labelled sentiment data for Positive / Negative / Neutral classification.
